Waffles

Description

A delicious dish to start your mornings off right!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Baking Soda
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Cinnamon (Optional)
  • Vanilla Extract

Steps

  1. In a large mixing bowl beat in eggs until yolk and white is fully mixed in
  2. Then add sugar, milk, vanilla extract and vegetable oil into bowl and mix until fully incorporated
  3. Afterward add flour and appropriate amount of baking soda into bowl and mix accordingly, folding in flour until no lumps remain
  4. Add cinnamon or any fragrant spices of your desire into waffle batter
  5. Prepare waffle iron with proper cooking setting and spray with cooking spray to ensure batter does not stick
  6. Use a ladle to scoop some of the batter into the waffle iron
  7. Cook until desired crispness and color
  8. Repeat process until batter is finished or desired amount of waffles
  9. Garnish with your favorite toppings and serve!
