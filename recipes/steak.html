Steak & Potatoes

Description

A hearty and filling plate for the last dish of the day!

Ingredients

  • Steak (Any cut of your choice
  • Potatoes
  • Garlic Glove
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Unsalted butter
  • Rosemary
  • Milk
  • Cumin
  1. Peel your potatoes and dice accordingly
  2. Fill a pot with cold water and season with salt, place potatoes into pot and boil under tender
  3. Season steak with salt and pepper
  4. Add tablespoon of olive oil into a saucepan and place on medium-high heat
  5. Lay steak into pan on oneside and then upon flipping place in small cubes of butter, rosemary and whole garlic clove
  6. Cook under desired color and texture
  7. Remove steak from pan and let cool for 10-15 min
  8. Prepare potatoes by draining pan and then adding milk, butter, salt, pepper and cumin and then mash until desired thickness and consistency
  9. Scoop mashed potatoes into the plate with the steak
  10. Serve with any side salad or greens and enjoy!
