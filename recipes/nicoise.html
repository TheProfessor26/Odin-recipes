Nicoise Salad

Description

A refreshing, colorful, and flavorful salad for your midday meal

Ingredients

  • Red Baby potatoes
  • Green Beans
  • A boiled egg
  • Cherry Tomatoes
  • Quality Tuna
  • Black Olives
  • Chopped Shallots
  • Lettuce
  • White Wine Vinegar
  • Sliced Radishes
  • Dijon Mustard
  • Chopped Fresh Thyme
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Olive Oil
  1. Place potatoes into a small pot filled with cold water and season with salt, bring to a simmer until potatoes are tender, then let cool
  2. In another pot with salted water bring green beans to a boil then drain and let cool
  3. Whisk the vinegar, mustard, thyme, salt and pepper into a separate bowl, then add the olive oil into dressing and slowly mix until fully emulsified
  4. Slice tomatoes and then toss in a small bowl with salt and pepper
  5. Quarter the hard boiled egg
  6. Assemble salad by first layering the bed of lettuce, then placing potatoes, green beans, radishes, eggs, tuna and tomatoes
  7. Finally drizzle salad with the dressing and garnish with the black olives
  8. Serve chilled and enjoy!
